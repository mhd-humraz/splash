Building a Better Internet for All

Mozilla is a global community of technologists, thinkers, and builders working together to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide can be informed contributors and creators of the Web.

Join the Movement

Privacy First

We believe your personal data belongs to you. Our products are designed to protect your privacy and give you control over your information.

Open Source

Our code is open for inspection, modification, and redistribution. We believe in transparency and community-driven development.

Accessibility for All

We work to ensure the internet remains a public resource that is open and accessible to everyone, regardless of location or ability.
